About

Student Construct is a project started by Ben Lloyd in order to aid the typical student with notetaking.
The website is free of charge, and caters for any subject. But what really is it?

A fluid, responsive, and exclusive notetaking website specified to your needs and preferences.
Designed by students, for students, with education in mind.
